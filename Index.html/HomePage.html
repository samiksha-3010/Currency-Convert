Even though primary care doctors treat some of the same injuries, it can be a good idea to see a doctor who’s an expert in treating these problems. Eligibility for Treatment

To determine eligibility for orthopedic treatment, it's essential to consider the nature of musculoskeletal problems. Orthopedic doctors specialize in treating injuries and conditions affecting the bones, joints, ligaments, muscles, and tendons.

  • Assessment of the specific musculoskeletal issue.
  • Previous treatments and their effectiveness.
  • Overall health and medical history.
